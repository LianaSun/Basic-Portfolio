About Me

Hi! I'm Liana Morrison and I'm currently enrolled at the Univeristy of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. I have an undergraduate degree in Dietetics and an Applied MBA in Small Business & Entreprenureship. For the last 12 years, my focus has been on managing yoga studios across the country. I've contracted with 25 studios nationwide, have taught over 6,000 public classes, 50+ workshops and private classes, including working for Lady Gaga and Peter Van Der Deen. I currently an partnered with a boutique studio in Minneapolis - Open Minds Fusion Studio and am on the founding board of the Minnesota Dancers Collective.

I'm mom to 5-year old Amelia and we currently live in St. Paul. Our family includes a corgi-mix dog named Penelope Jane (Penny) and a Western Hognose Snake named Magical Stryker.
